Add or pre-save to library/playlist

If pre-saves are enabled and your release date is in the future, your fans can pre-save your music before it's out. Any other store links added will display as "Pre-order" links until the specified release date. Your music will be automatically added to the libraries of all your fans who pre-saved it on the day of the release!

After the release all other store links will also be listed on your smart link along with buttons to let new visitors add your music to their library and playlists. You can leave the links empty and still collect pre-saves, but you will have to enter them on release date.

If the release date is in the past or left blank we will ask fans to directly add your music to their library or playlists.

AFTER RELEASE DATE

Fans will also be asked to follow your artist profile on Spotify and Deezer when saving your music as long as you have entered your ID information here.
